1. Intra-sentence self-attention
Parikh et al. (2016) "A Decomposable Attention Model for NLI"; Wang & Jiang (2017)

Add a self-attention pass within each sentence before cross-attention, so each token attends to its own sentence first.

2. Label Smoothing
Szegedy et al. (2016) "Rethinking the Inception Architecture"; Müller et al. (2019)

Replace CrossEntropyLoss with CrossEntropyLoss(label_smoothing=0.1). One-line change.

3. Attention-weighted pooling ADDED
Bahdanau et al. (2015); dos Santos et al. (2016) "Attentive Pooling Networks"

Add a learned attention-weighted sum pooling alongside the existing max+mean in StockClassifier.

4. Variational dropout in BiLSTMs
Gal & Ghahramani (2016) "A Theoretically Grounded Application of Dropout in RNNs"

Apply the same dropout mask across all timesteps instead of a different one per step — stronger regularisation for LSTMs.

5. Stochastic depth
Huang et al. (2016) "Deep Networks with Stochastic Depth"

Randomly skip entire ESIM blocks during training (e.g. p=0.1 per block). Strong regulariser for deep residual networks.

6. Highway input projection: ADDED
Srivastava et al. (2015) "Training Very Deep Networks"

Replace the linear input projection with a gated highway network — useful given the heterogeneous 1477-d input.
